How our match selection works!

1 Mark Attendence!

First step to start a match is to mark your attendence so that our Admins would know that you are online!

2 Show Interest!

Then you have to show an interest in a game that you want to play and also the player that you want to play against!

3 Team You up!

Then Admin looks over proper ranking and assign you a session either against a player or a coach based on your skills!

Our scoring system!

This is the scoring system that we believe in and stay true to!

Beginner

Rank 0

The starting rank that a player should assign to themself and rank up as they play more!

Medium

Rank 1

This rank means you are getting serios and moving up the ladder to play against more competitive players!

Advance

Rank 2

Only the pros and prodigies can reach such level and keep on it!
